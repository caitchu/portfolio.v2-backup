About

Hi there! I'm Caitlin, a graphic designer & overseas teacher turned front-end developer & web designer. I'm a quick learner and my peers describe me as detail-oriented, observant and investigative.

Creating interactive experiences that are user-friendly, minimal and engaging means a lot to me, especially when they benefit people's lives. I pour a lot of care into what I craft, and also have a deeply growth-oriented attitude. I love learning, and often seek environments that encourage and embrace it too.

Outside of design & coding, I'm an avid karaoke goer and music dabbler. I also like nature spots, psychology & well-being books, and video games.
