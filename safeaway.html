SafeAway

A personal project done to help traveling Canadians look up advisories for their destination country of choice for safer and well-informed travel.

  • Role: Front-end Developer, Web Designer
  • Tools Used: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Sass, API
View Live

The Problem

Canadians who are traveling to other countries often have trouble getting updates through their embassy or even the internet alone to determine the safety index of their destinations.

The Solution

Design and develop a website that can supply information on destination countries including risks of disease, disaster, crime, threats, ongoing political concerns and other safety hazards.
